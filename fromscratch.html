Grizzly Bears

The Grizzly Bear, Ursus arctos horribilis, the greatest predator in North America.

My Professional Page
  1. Size
    • The average female Grizzly bear weighs 290lbs-400lbs
    • The average male Grizzly bear weighs 400lbs-700lbs
    • Can reach up to 8ft in height
  2. Diet
    • Grizzly bears are omnivores
    • Grizzlies eat berries, plants, nuts, herbs, roots, along with fish(salmon), rodents, deer, elk, moose, and other animals
  3. Features
    • Great sense of smell
    • Can run up to speeds of 40mph
    • Extremely strong
    • Claws range from 2-4 inches long
